404 Page

For an assignment in the course 'Integration of Advanced Frontend Development,' we designed and developed a 404 page for our lab project's website. My lab project focused on assisting people with anxiety disorders. The game requires users to press the circles, the circles show you how to breathe. Playing this game can help people breathe and relax.

Required components:

  • Utilizing CSS animations, transitions, and/or JavaScript animations (such as a mini-game).
  • Incorporating the concept of our digital product.
  • Integrating the visual identity of our digital product into the design.
  • Providing a link back to the website's homepage.
  • Explaining to the visitor what the 404 error signifies.
