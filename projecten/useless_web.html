Useless website

In 2012, Tim Holman started his project The Useless Web, a collection of completely useless websites. In the Design 4 course, we were tasked to create our own XD version of The Useless Web. The only criterion was that the idea had to be completely useless.

How did I approach it?

  • Exploration:

    In the exploration phase, I began with research on the original The Useless Web project and other similar useless websites. I analyzed the types of useless interactions and concepts that were popular and brainstormed how I could contribute something unique.

  • Execution:

    I started by brainstorming and coding different ideas. I created a color picker, an endless loader, and a page that only displays a construction message. Then, I combined these to make a better whole.
