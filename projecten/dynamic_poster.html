Dynamic Poster

For the Design 4 course, we were tasked with creating a dynamic poster themed around Christopher Nolan's films. We were given several options, and I chose to base my poster on "Batman: The Dark Knight". The goal was to create a poster that captures the essence of the film and has even more impact through movement.

How did I approach it?

  • Exploration:

    In the exploration phase, I started with thorough research on "The Dark Knight" and Christopher Nolan's distinctive visual style. I analyzed key themes, iconic images, and color palettes from the film.

  • Execution:

    The execution phase involved developing and refining the design for the dynamic poster. I began with sketches and then created a static version of the poster, after which I added the dynamic elements.
