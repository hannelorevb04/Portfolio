Benefits

Unique for your needs

Creating an account, let us help you with your specific needs. You can select in which we can help you. Based on this, the app will be specified for you.

Meditation & personal messages

Listening to sounds that help you relax. With the help of messages from family and friends, you feel less alone and learn to overcome your fears.

Relax & SOS

When you have an anxiety attack, you can quickly call family members or friends, allowing them to reassure you. If they are not available or you can't call, you can always use the relax function, which helps you breathe in and out calmly.
