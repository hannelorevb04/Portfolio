Woordlogo

In the Design 4 course, we were assigned to transform a word into a wordlogo. The challenge was to incorporate a visual reference to the word's meaning within the wordlogo. Additionally, we had to consider how to add motion to further enhance the word's meaning.

How did we approach it?

  • Exploration:

    In the exploration phase, I began brainstorming visual references that could enhance this meaning. I studied different fonts and observed how subtle adjustments in letterforms could express the essence of the word.

  • Execution:

    The execution phase involved developing the wordlogo, including choosing the font, applying necessary manipulations, and determining the colors and background.
